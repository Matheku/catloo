Frequently Asked Questions

Is the CatLoo safe for all cats?

Yes! Our toilets are designed for cats 8 weeks and older, up to 20 pounds. The step-by-step training guide ensures a smooth transition from litter boxes.

How does the flushing mechanism work?

The Basic and Pro models use a simple button-activated flush system. The Deluxe model features motion sensors that detect when your cat has finished.

What plumbing connections are needed?

Standard 1.25-inch connections included. Compatible with most residential plumbing. Professional installation recommended for Deluxe model smart features.

How often does it need cleaning?

The self-cleaning cycle on Deluxe model runs automatically. Basic/Pro models need manual cleaning once weekly with included non-abrasive cleaner.

What's the warranty period?

All models include our 3-year comprehensive warranty covering manufacturing defects, with lifetime support for all customers.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide with 3-5 business day processing and 7-21 day delivery depending on location. Free shipping on orders over $150.
